About CSE Department


Overview

The Department of Computer Science & Engineering at University of Barishal is committed to providing top-notch education, fostering research, and creating leaders in the tech industry. Established with a mission to bridge academia and industry, the department focuses on innovative teaching methodologies, modern laboratories, and collaborative projects.

Vision

Become a center of excellence in computing education and research, empowering students to innovate and lead globally.

Mission

  • Provide quality undergraduate and postgraduate education in CSE.
  • Foster research and innovation in emerging computing areas.
  • Collaborate with industry for practical and impactful solutions.
  • Encourage entrepreneurship and societal contributions through technology.

Core Values

Innovation, Integrity, Collaboration, Diversity, Academic Excellence, Social Responsibility.

Department Facilities

  • State-of-the-art computer labs equipped with modern hardware and software.
  • Research labs for AI, IoT, Cybersecurity, and Networking.
  • Library with latest books, journals, and online resources.
  • Student clubs, hackathons, workshops, and seminars to encourage hands-on learning.
