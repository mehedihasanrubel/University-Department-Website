About the Department

The Department of Computer Science & Engineering at University of Barishal focuses on education, research, and innovation, preparing students for the global tech world.

Research Areas

Internet of Things

Our IoT Lab focuses on developing smart, interconnected devices and sensor networks. Research areas include real-time monitoring, IoT security, automation systems, and innovative applications for healthcare, agriculture, and smart cities.

Networks & Cybersecurity

High-speed networks, cloud security, intrusion detection, and secure systems research.

Digital Logic Design

The DLD Lab offers hands-on experience with digital circuits, logic gates, and sequential designs for practical learning
